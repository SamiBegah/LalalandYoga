ABOUT US

"Welcome to Lalaland Yoga, a sanctuary of tranquility and balance, conceptualized and brought to life by Anastasia K and Laudy F. From the hustle and bustle of everyday life, we realized the need for a peaceful retreat - a place where people could reconnect with themselves and others, thus birthing Lalaland Yoga. Our retreats, spread across the world's most enchanting locations, provide a harmonious blend of yoga, meditation, and fun-filled activities like body painting, fostering a vibrant community spirit. Anastasia, having devoted over a decade to yoga, imparts a wealth of knowledge and passion, creating a deeply enriching yoga experience.


Laudy, on the other hand, excels in meticulously crafting and managing each retreat, ensuring every participant feels cherished and rejuvenated. At Lalaland Yoga, we are more than a retreat: we are a community that welcomes yogis of all levels, including children. Our mission is to inspire mindfulness, self-love, and relaxation, whilst our vision is to touch as many lives as possible, helping individuals find their inner peace amidst life's chaos. Join us at Lalaland Yoga on this enlightening journey of self-discovery and let's create cherished memories together in some of the most idyllic corners of the Earth. We look forward to welcoming you into our global Lalaland Yoga family." - Lalaland Yoga's team.

OUR TEAM

Anastasia K.

Anastasia fell in Love with yoga in 2011. She decided to go deeper in her practice, went all the way to India to get certified for 500 RYT hour and became a teacher 5 years ago. She is trained in Hatha, Vinyasa Flow, AcroYoga, restorative and Yin Yoga. She is consistently acquiring knowledge in the science and philosophy behind yoga with her teacher from India.

Laudy F.

Laudy's expertise shines brightly in planning enriching yoga retreats with her partner, Anastasia. She finds joy, peace and spiritual growth in her yoga journey. An ardent admirer of Yoga and Vinyasa Flow, she also keenly explores other styles. She values continuous learning, always deepening her understanding of yoga philosophy and science with her mentor.
