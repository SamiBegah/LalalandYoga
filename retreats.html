WE ARE COMING TO LEBANON!

Come and join us on this amazing wellness retreat. Disconnect to better reconnect through the practice of yoga, meditation, mindfulness and time spent with mother nature. Offer yourself a break from daily life and focus on your physical, mental & spiritual health. Immerse yourself in an authentic and raw inward journey in the beautiful mountains of Lebanon. Allow Your mind, body and soul to absorb the peaceful beauty of nature and relax to its sound. It's a great way to connect with yourself and likeminded people.

You will be able to attend amazing lessons with our experienced Yoga Teacher who will lead different classes & workshops on different styles of yoga, meditation & mindfulness. You Will be offered a Full body Swedish or deep tissue massage by a certified massage therapist to help you rejuvenate. You will also be able to enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, off road ATV riding, crafting, watching the beautiful Sunrise and Sunset in the mountains, stargazing, etc.

You will be staying in a stunning chalet resort on a natural reserve and daily experience a succulent variety of home cooked Lebanese cuisine made with only organic locally gown products. A fully Vegan Menu will also be available. Finally, you will end the week Beautifully by giving back to nature and planting your own sacred juniper tree. A portion of the profits will be donated to the amazing organization that is protecting and taking care of the reserve since the year 2000.
