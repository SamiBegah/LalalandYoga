Yoga Styles

Hatha Yoga

Focusing on physical postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama), Hatha Yoga incorporates a combination of gentle and dynamic movements to harmonize and balance the body and mind. Promotes:

#alignment #strength #flexibility #relaxation

Vinyasa Yoga

Involving a dynamic and flowing style of yoga that synchronizes breath with movement, Vinyasa Yoga consist of smoothly transitioning from one pose to another in a sequence, creating a continuous and rhythmic flow. Promotes:

#strength #flexibility #mindfulness #breathing

Yin Yoga

Being a slow-paced and meditative style of yoga, Yin Yoga involves long-held passive poses targeting deep connective tissues to practice stillness and introspection. Promotes:

#relaxation #flexibility #awareness

Acroyoga

Acroyoga is a fusion of yoga, acrobatic that cultivates balance, flexibility, and strength through collaboration and trust between partners. Promotes:

#balance #flexibility #strength #trust
